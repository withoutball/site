Capabilities

Powered by advanced AI.

Six core capabilities that transform raw match footage into actionable intelligence, from skeleton tracking to predictive analytics.

Pose Estimation

17-keypoint skeletal tracking for every player on the pitch at 25 fps, powered by our custom transformer architecture.

Movement Signatures

Unique motion fingerprints that characterize off-ball behavior, enabling comparison across leagues and seasons.

Tactical Clustering

Automatically group and classify defensive structures, pressing patterns, and space-occupation strategies.

Spatial Intelligence

Heat maps, Voronoi diagrams, and pitch-control models that quantify space creation and defensive coverage.

Predictive Runs

Forecast the next 2-3 seconds of player trajectories to anticipate runs, overlaps, and pressing triggers.

Match Readiness

Evaluate physical and tactical readiness scores based on movement intensity, positioning discipline, and fatigue markers.

Under the Hood

Built on cutting-edge research.

Our platform leverages the latest advances in computer vision, self-supervised learning, and real-time systems engineering.

Architecture

Pose Transformer

Custom spatiotemporal transformer that processes 17-keypoint skeletal sequences, learning rich representations of player body language and movement intent.

Transformer Self-Attention Positional Encoding
Pre-training

Masked Joint Modeling

Self-supervised pre-training on thousands of match sequences using masked joint prediction, learning to reconstruct missing pose information.

MJM SSL Reconstruction
Representation

Contrastive Learning

VICReg and Barlow Twins objectives create invariant representations that capture tactical similarity regardless of camera angle or player identity.

VICReg Barlow Twins Embeddings
Deployment

Real-Time Pipeline

End-to-end inference pipeline optimized for live match analysis with sub-40ms latency per frame, from video input to tactical output.
